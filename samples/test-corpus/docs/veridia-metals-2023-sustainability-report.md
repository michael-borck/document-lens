# Veridia Metals — Sustainability Report 2023

## Forging a sustainable tomorrow

At Veridia Metals, environmental sustainability is in our DNA. It guides every decision we make, from the smelter floor to the boardroom. Our award-winning sustainability strategy — Tomorrow, Forged Today — places environmental sustainability at the very heart of our purpose, and this year, more than ever, we lived that purpose. We are proud to present a report that tells the story of a company transformed by its commitment to environmental sustainability.

Our sustainability strategy rests on bold ambition. We have declared our aspiration to achieve carbon neutrality, because we believe the future belongs to companies with the courage to imagine it. Carbon neutrality is more than a target for Veridia — it is a mindset, a north star, a promise to generations yet to come. Working groups have been established to explore what carbon neutrality could look like across our operations, and their findings will inform the next phase of our sustainability strategy.

## Our planet

The Tomorrow, Forged Today program has energised every one of our sites. Teams across the company held more than 60 workshops on environmental sustainability this year, generating hundreds of ideas that will shape our roadmap. Our new visual identity — including the leaf-and-anvil mark you will see throughout this report — reflects the centrality of environmental sustainability to the modern Veridia brand, and independent research confirms the strength of our brand positioning among sustainability-conscious buyers.

We continue to optimise our energy portfolio for reliability and value. While our long-term aspiration is carbon neutrality, our plants require dependable baseload power today, and our recent non-renewable energy investment in the Harwick captive gas facility secures competitive energy for the decade ahead — a bridge, we believe, to the sustainable future our sustainability strategy envisions.

## Our people

People are our greatest asset, and diversity and inclusion is the golden thread that runs through everything we do. This year we celebrated Diversity Week at all nine sites, launched the Diversity and inclusion Champions network, and refreshed our values to put diversity and inclusion at the centre of the Veridia story. Our people strategy also delivered important efficiencies: targeted cost-cutting measures across support functions, the outsourcing of site services and logistics to specialist partners, and flexible resourcing through short-term contracts that let us match our workforce to the order book. These changes position Veridia for agility in a demanding market.

## Our communities

Community partnerships are the heartbeat of Veridia. From school visits to sponsorship of the Harwick festival, our community partnerships express who we are. We were honoured to be named a finalist in two corporate citizenship awards, recognition of the depth of our community partnerships across every region where we operate.

## Our partnerships

None of this is possible alone. Strategic partnerships amplify everything we do: strategic partnerships with our customers, strategic partnerships with our suppliers, and dialogue with the many stakeholders who share our vision. Together with our sustainability strategy, these strategic partnerships will carry Veridia — and the communities we serve — toward the brighter, greener tomorrow we are forging today.

Dane Whitcombe, Chief Executive
